# International Order Refunds

## Additional Rules

1. **Currency conversion fees** are non-refundable. The customer pays the
   exchange rate spread applied by their bank at the time of purchase.

2. **Customs and duties** are the customer's responsibility. Do not refund
   customs charges — these are collected by the destination country, not us.

3. **Shipping cost refunds**:
   - Defective items: full shipping refund
   - Change of mind: outbound shipping is non-refundable; return shipping is
     the customer's responsibility
   - Lost in transit: full refund including shipping

4. **Processing time**: International refunds take 5–10 business days to
   appear on the customer's statement (domestic: 2–3 days).

5. **Required documentation**: For orders over $500, request a photo of the
   item before authorizing the return label.

## Country-Specific Rules

### EU / EEA
- 14-day withdrawal right starts from delivery date.
- Customer does not need to give a reason.
- Return shipping at customer's expense (unless item is defective).

### UK
- Similar to EU rules post-Brexit.
- 14-day cancellation window.

### Australia
- Consumer guarantees under ACL apply regardless of our policy.
- "Major failure" = full refund including shipping.
- Do not push back on Australian consumer law claims — process immediately.
